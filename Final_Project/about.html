About

Philesq Studio

Based in Los Angeles, Philesq Studio connects with others through concept and form, while also forcing hand-made and digital mediums to live in tension. Works often features uncontrollability and ex-plorations of various mediums. Philesq Studio specializes in design for both culture and popular culture; music, art and architecture are all areas that either influence him or are incorporated into its work.

Louis Alvarado

Louis Alvarado is a Los Angeles based transmedia designer, whose work spans a variety of platforms. Since receiving his BFA from Otis College of Art and Design in 2014, he has worked alongside influential design figure April Greiman, and started his own studio practice, Philesq.
